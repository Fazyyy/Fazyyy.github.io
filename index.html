My name is Jaimie, I live on the edge of Cornwall and Plymouth and I make things on the internet using HTML, CSS and Javascript along with various frameworks. I love the creativity involved with the creation of the website and designing how the different responsive and interactive elements of any site might work. I'm always looking for a challenge.

I have several years experience which has given me the experience to quickly look at a design and quickly get to work developing the design in an efficient and responsive manner.

When I'm not busy typing away at a computer, I also enjoy photography, gaming, traveling, playing guitar and surfing.

As a developer in a Web team I have been part of an agile team of developers and testers, developing top of class software built within a custom ASP.NET CMS using Web Forms. I have been involved in all stages of development including the analysis of business requiremens, planning, implementation and delivery. I am comfortable working as part of a team or autonomosly and have experience working both locally and remotely.

In my role as a developer I find that frameworks are an amazing tool for not only coding more efficiently but also to enable you to support a wider range of devices with very little effort, some of my favourite frameworks include Bootstrap, Jquery and SASS, although I have experience working with many more.

As a web developer it is almost inevitable that you will be required to do some minor work with graphics and as such I have gained experience and proficiency working with image editing tools such as GIMP, Adobe Photoshop and Invision.
